# -Perpetual-calendar-
A perpetual calendar is a calendar valid for many years, usually designed to allow
the calculation of the day of the week for a given date in the future.
The programm to create on C++.
You are enter month(February or 2), day(1,2,3,etc), year(1583-9999) and you out day of weeek(Monday, Sunday, etc).
I am used formula of Zeller's congruence(https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zeller%27s_congruence).
